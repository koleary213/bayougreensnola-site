Simple & Stress-Free

How It Works

Every course is built unique to your event. We work with you to choose the best holes for your crowd — then handle everything else.

1
We Design It Together

Tell us your event date, location, and who's coming. We help you build a course that's unique to your event — choosing the best holes for your crowd, whether it's kids, coworkers, or wedding guests.

2
We Deliver & Set Up

Our team arrives ahead of your event and builds your custom course — leveled, styled, and ready to play.

3
You Play

Enjoy your event while guests tee off. No setup, no cleanup, no stress on your end.

4
We Break It Down

When the fun wraps, we return to tear everything down and haul it away. Easy as that.
