Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve, and when are you available?
We bring the course to you across Greater New Orleans — including New Orleans, Metairie, Kenner, and nearby communities — seven days a week. Planning something a little farther out? Just ask and we'll let you know if we can make it work.
Can you set up indoors or outdoors?
Both! Our courses work great in backyards, driveways, patios, and parks, or indoors in gyms, halls, and event spaces. We just need a reasonably flat, clean surface — grass, turf, concrete, and asphalt all work well (bare dirt doesn't).
How much space do you need?
As a rule of thumb, plan for about 4 ft × 10 ft per hole so players have room to move around each one. Layout is flexible — we can group the holes in one big area or spread them across rooms, hallways, or a yard. Tell us your space and we'll design the course to fit.
Can I choose which holes I get and how hard they are?
Absolutely — and that's what makes us different. You pick how many holes and the difficulty mix: Par 3 (easy), Par 4 (medium), Par 5 (hard), and specialty trick holes. We help you design a course that's just right for your crowd — little kids, competitive coworkers, or wedding guests. Every Bayou Greens course is one of a kind.
How many holes can I get?
Anywhere from 1 to 18 holes. Not sure what's right? A 9-hole course is our most popular for most parties, while 18 holes is great for festivals and large crowds. We'll help you choose.
What's included with a rental?
Every package includes delivery, professional setup, and breakdown, plus adult and kid-size putters, golf balls, scorecards, and pencils — and up to 3 hours of play. You don't lift a finger.
What kinds of events do you do?
Just about any gathering! We're a favorite for kids' and adult birthday parties, corporate events and team-building, company picnics, weddings and receptions, school and church functions, fundraisers, festivals, grand openings, and neighborhood block parties across Greater New Orleans. And because every course is custom-built, we tailor the holes and difficulty to match your crowd — from a backyard full of kids to a corporate happy hour.
Is it fun for all ages?
Absolutely — mini golf is a hit with every age, from toddlers to grandparents. We bring toddler, kid, and adult-size putters so everyone can play comfortably, and because you pick the mix of hole difficulties (easy Par 3s up to challenging Par 5s and specialty holes), there's something fun for the little ones and the competitive grown-ups alike. It's one of the few activities that genuinely brings the whole crowd together.
What if it rains?
Mini golf plays best in dry weather. If rain is in the forecast, we'll work with you on a plan — moving the course indoors or under cover where possible, or finding another date. We'll keep an eye on the forecast with you as your event approaches.
How far in advance should I book?
The earlier the better, especially for weekend dates, which fill up fast. If you'd like custom-printed golf balls or a banner, please book with extra lead time so we can have them made.
How do I book, and how does the deposit work?
Message us with your event date and details and we'll confirm availability and send a quote. A $100 deposit holds your date and is applied in full toward your total; the balance is due around your event. We're happy to invoice corporate and school clients.
Can I get custom golf balls for my event?
Yes! Add our Custom Event Package — a 2 ft × 4 ft event banner plus 30 custom-printed golf balls your guests can take home. It's a favorite for weddings, corporate events, and milestone parties. Just order early, since custom printing takes lead time.

Still have a question? Message us — we're happy to help.
